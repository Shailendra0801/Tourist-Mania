Why Visit Nainital?

Nestled in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, Nainital is a serene hill station known for its scenic beauty, colonial charm, and vibrant culture. Surrounded by lush green hills and centered around the sparkling Naini Lake, Nainital offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and urban comforts.

Must-Visit Attractions

Discover the most enchanting spots in Nainital, from serene lakes to breathtaking viewpoints, each offering unique experiences and unforgettable memories.

Naini Lake

The heart of the town. Boating on this lake surrounded by hills is a must-do experience that captures the essence of Nainital.

Naina Devi Temple

A revered Shakti Peeth located by the lake, offering spiritual peace and panoramic views of the surrounding hills.

Snow View Point

Offers panoramic views of the Himalayas. Accessible by cable car, providing an unforgettable journey to stunning vistas.

The Mall Road

A bustling street full of cafes, shops, and local handicrafts. Perfect for shopping and experiencing local culture.

Tiffin Top (Dorothy's Seat)

A popular picnic spot with great views, perfect for spending quality time with family and friends.

GB Pant High Altitude Zoo

Home to endangered species like the snow leopard and Himalayan bear. A must-visit for wildlife enthusiasts.

Local Flavors

Savor the authentic taste of Kumaoni cuisine and popular local delicacies that will tantalize your taste buds and give you a true flavor of the region.

Aloo Ke Gutke

Spiced boiled potatoes, a staple of Kumaoni homes with authentic mountain flavors.

Bhatt Ki Churkani

A traditional black bean curry that's both nutritious and delicious.

Jholi

A thin curry made with curd and besan (gram flour) with spices.

Bal Mithai

A popular local sweet made of khoya and sugar balls, a must-try delicacy.

Momos & Maggi

Found at almost every corner, especially near viewpoints for quick bites.
